VTK supports a wide variety of visualization  algorithms including: scalar, vector, tensor, texture, and volumetric methods; and advanced modeling techniques  such as: implicit modeling, polygon reduction, mesh smoothing, cutting, contouring, and Delaunay triangulation.Homepage: http://www.vtk.org
